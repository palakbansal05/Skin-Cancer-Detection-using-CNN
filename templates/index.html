What is Skin Cancer?

Skin cancer happens when abnormal cells grow uncontrollably. Skin Cancer is one of the most abundant types of cancer in this time.

Risk Factors

Factors include genetics, sunlight, and lifestyle. Constant exposure to sunlight is a vital and growing cause of skin cancer in people now days.

Types of Skin Cancer

Melanoma, basal cell carcinoma, and squamous cell carcinoma are some types of skin cancer. Among the three of them, melanoma is the most dangerous one.

ABCDE Rule

A simple rule to identify suspicious moles.

Prevention Tips

Sun protection is the best thing one can do to prevent themselves from skin Cancer. Regular checkups are extremely beneficial when it comes to prevention of skin Cancer.

When to See a Doctor

Seek medical advice if changes in the lesion occur and the lesions are permanent from a long period of time.
